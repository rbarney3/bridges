All About Bridges!

What Makes a Strong Bridge?

Triangles

Triangles are very strong shapes. They help spread out the weight so bridges don’t fall down!

Materials

Bridges are built with strong materials like steel, stone, and concrete.

Support

Bridges need supports or towers to hold up the road or walkway.

Types of Bridges

Beam Bridge

Beam bridges are simple, flat bridges. They work well for short distances.

Arch Bridge

Arch bridges are curved. The shape helps spread out the weight and makes them strong.

Suspension Bridge

Suspension bridges hang from tall towers with cables. They can stretch across very long distances!
